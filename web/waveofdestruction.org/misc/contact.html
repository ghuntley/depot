About Waveofdestruction.org

On the 28th WOD was created to serve as a central location for videos/photos related to the tsunami.

Everything was peachy until the 29th when everything went bottom-side up.

To be honest I wasnt prepared for the sheer amount of traffic that came and the server crashed many times that night.

You can actually see the traffic pickup and then the downtime in the network graphs.


From 39,000 Page serves to 1.2 Million in just under 24 hours.

After a lot of hair pulling, tweaking and a few sacrifices (photogallery is no longer live and updates take 6 hours to show up) the server is now stable even at the current rate of 2.5M / day.
